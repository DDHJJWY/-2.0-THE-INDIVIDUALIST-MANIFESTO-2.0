## ⚡ Core Concepts / 宣言核心

### 1. Cognitive Sovereignty / 认知主权
**You do not own what you cannot defend.** In the wilderness of information, only logic is your defense.
**你并不拥有你无法防御的东西。** 在信息的荒原中，只有逻辑是你的防线。

### 2. Logical Authority / 逻辑权威
**Strip away all emotional parasitism; reject moral kidnapping.** Causality is the only judge in the universe.
**剥离一切情感的寄生，拒绝道德绑架。** 因果律是宇宙中唯一的审判官。

### 3. Power Justice / 实力正义
**Abandon the illusion of hypocritical contracts.** Between individuals, the balance of power is the most stable peace agreement.
**放弃对虚伪契约的幻想。** 在个体之间，实力的平衡才是最稳固的和平协议。
